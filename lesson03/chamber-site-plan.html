Salt Lake Valley Chamber - Site Plan

I decided to end the name with just "Chamber" because Salt Lake Valley is a lot. So adding Chamber of commerce fully felt like to much, but ending with "Chamber" makes it a nice length.

Purpose

The purpose of the Salt lake Valley Chamber of Commerce site is to provide information for the Salt Lake Valley, Utah area. This site will provide information that will strenghthen the community. This site will display upcoming events for the area, help small businesses, and provide helpful sources of information for the community to keep them up to date with what is going on in the valley.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find a calendar of the upcoming events?
  • What is the latest news about the area I am living in?
  • Where can I find a list of small businesses in the community to support?
  • Where can I find information on the chamber of commerce committee?
  • Where are some new places to eat?
