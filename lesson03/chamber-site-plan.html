Sandy Chamber of Commerce - Site Plan

Purpose

The purpose of the Sandy Chamber of Commerce site is to provide information to the city of Sandy, Utah. This site will provide information that will strenghthen the community. This site will display upcoming events for the city, help small businesses, and provide helpful sources of information for the community to keep them up to date with the city.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find a calendar of the upcoming events?
  • What is the latest news about the city I am living in?
  • Where can I find a list of small businesses in the community to support?
  • Where can I find information on the chamber of commerce committee?
